Alphabet: 
	- upper (A-Z) and lower case letters (a-z) of the English alphabet
	- underline character '_'
	- decimal digits (0-9)

Lexic:
	- special symbols, representing:
		* operators: lt le eq ge gt > + - * / mod
		* separators: ;[...]; ;; space
		* reserved symbols: > -> ? : .
		* reserved words: let num wrd arr
	- identifiers:
		* a sequence of letters, digits and underline characters, such that the first character is a letter or an underline character
			letter 			- (a-z)|(A-Z)
			digit 			- (0-9)
			underline character 	- _
	- constants:
		* integer: 		"+"nr | "-"nr | nr > cnum
		* character/string: 	'letter/string' | 'digit/number' > cwrd
